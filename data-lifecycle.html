    %% UI → Store
    Dashboard --> CS
    CoursePage --> CS
    StudentPage --> SS
    etc --> ES & MS

    %% Store → Helper
    CS & SS & ES & MS -->|"helper.load\nadd / update / remove"| Load & Add & Update & Remove

    %% Helper → 분기
    Load & Add & Update & Remove --> CloudCheck

    %% Cloud 경로
    CloudCheck -->|"YES"| SLoad & SInsert & SUpdate & SDelete
    SLoad --> FromDb --> SupaDB
    SInsert --> ToDb --> SupaDB
    SUpdate --> ToDb
    SDelete --> SupaDB

    %% Local 경로
    CloudCheck -->|"NO"| LD & ATS & UIS & DFS
    LD & ATS & UIS & DFS --> Session

    %% 퍼시스턴스
    Session -->|"persist"| TauriCheck
    TauriCheck -->|"YES — 데스크톱"| TauriFS
    TauriCheck -->|"NO — 브라우저"| LSFallback
    TauriFS -.->|"실패 시 폴백"| LSFallback
